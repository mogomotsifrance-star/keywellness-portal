🔒 Data Protection & Confidentiality

Please read and accept our data protection statement before accessing the portal.

How We Protect Your Information

Key Wellness is committed to protecting your personal and financial data. By using this portal, you agree to the following:

  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers (Supabase / PostgreSQL) and is accessible only by you and authorised Key Wellness advisors.
  • Purpose Limitation: Your information is used exclusively for financial wellness coaching, assessments, and personalised recommendations. It will not be used for any other purpose.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your personal and financial data will not be sold, rented, or shared with any third parties outside of Key Wellness without your explicit written consent.
  • Advisor Access: Your assigned Key Wellness coach may view your wellness scores, goals, and assessment results to provide coaching support. This is limited to service delivery only.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to request access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, and deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting wellness@keywellness.co.bw.
  • Botswana Data Protection: This portal operates in accordance with the data protection principles of the Republic of Botswana and applicable privacy legislation.
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days.
