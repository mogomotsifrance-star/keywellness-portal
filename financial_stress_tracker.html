Coping Strategies
📋
Write It Down
List all your financial obligations. Uncertainty is often more stressful than the actual numbers.
🎯
One Step Today
Pick one small financial action — pay one bill, call one creditor. Progress beats paralysis.
🧘
Separate Emotion
Money stress activates fight-or-flight. Deep breathing before reviewing finances helps decision quality.
🤝
Talk to Someone
A financial advisor, counsellor, or trusted friend can provide perspective you can't see alone.
📅
Schedule Money Time
Set one 30-min slot per week for finances. Containing it stops financial anxiety from bleeding into daily life.
🌱
Emergency Fund First
Even P500 in savings reduces stress significantly. Small buffers break the paycheck-to-paycheck cycle.
