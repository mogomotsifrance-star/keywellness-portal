Types of Life Insurance in Botswana
Term Life
Covers a fixed period (e.g. 20 years). Pure protection — no cash value. Usually the most affordable option.
Whole Life
Covers your entire life and builds cash value over time. Higher premiums but includes a savings component.
Group Cover
Often provided by employers in Botswana. Usually 2–4× annual salary. Check if it's portable if you leave your job.
